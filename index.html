About Me

Hey there! I’m Dian Andrae Sanchez, and welcome to my portfolio. I grew up in Pampanga, where I learned the importance of love, care, respect, responsibility, trust, and loyalty. For me, loyalty isn’t something you can take for granted—it’s a two-way street. If you can’t offer it to me, don’t expect it back. Responsibility goes hand-in-hand with loyalty. To me, it’s all about owning your actions and being accountable for what you do.


Here in Pampanga, I’ve got a few go-to spots, especially for my late-night rides. I love grabbing a cold coffee in a quiet, cozy corner where I can just think about the future and what’s next. I tend to stay longer than I plan, just reflecting on things. But I’m rarely alone—being by myself feels too quiet. So, I always invite my friends who are free or up for joining me. On weekends, my friends and I usually set up get-togethers, whether it's just to bond or play sports.


With all that going on, I still find time to play online games. I consider it a waste not to use my talent for gaming. The thing is, I’m pretty good at almost any game I pick up. If I enjoy it, I often end up ranking high. But, of course, it can be time-consuming, and I’ve noticed it’s taken a toll on my health.


While I enjoy my downtime, I also make sure to maintain a healthy lifestyle. I try to go to the gym almost every day, whether it's for a workout, a run, or a game of basketball. Staying active is important to me. But when it comes to eating healthy, I’ll admit—it’s a struggle. Even though I don’t have to strictly watch what I eat, it’s still tough to make the best food choices consistently. Finding balance between enjoying life and staying healthy is a challenge, but I’m always working on it.
